W05 Project: Website Planning Document

This planning document outlines the concept, audience needs, and design direction for the Liberians Dishes website.

Liberians Dishes Website

Liberians Dishes a celebration of Liberia's most loved home and roadside meals and the stories behind them.

The name is direct and memorable. It clearly tells visitors they will find authentic, everyday Liberian dishes and where to experience them.

Optional domain: liberiansdishes.com

Logo Concept: A fork and bowl icon to represent everyday dishes, paired with the Liberian flag colors for a proud, recognizable identity.

Site Purpose

The Liberian Dishes website highlights popular local dishes, where to find them, and tips for first-time visitors. It will serve as a guide for locals and travelers who want quick, trusted recommendations and cultural context for each meal.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the best jollof rice and grilled fish stalls in Monrovia?
  • What dishes are safe for first-time visitors who are sensitive to spice?
  • How much should I expect to pay for common local dishes?

Color Schema

The palette reflects the Liberian flag colors: bold red, deep blue, and crisp white. These colors are applied to headings, navigation, callouts, and accents throughout the site.

Liberian Red

Hex: #bf0a30

Use: Headings, navigation bar, and emphasis text.

Liberian Blue

Hex: #002868

Use: Buttons, highlights, icons, and badges.

Liberian White

Hex: #ffffff

Use: Backgrounds, spacing areas, and clean contrast.

Typography

The typography pairing balances a classic serif headline font with a clean, readable sans-serif for body content.

  • Merriweather: Headings and hero titles.
  • Source Sans 3: Body text, navigation, and supporting details.
